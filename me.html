Crochet Lover/ Heavy Sleeper / Amateur Yogi / Unapologetically Asian

Just to reintroduce myself, my name is Gina Hsu and I’m a product designer. I studied Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo with a double minor in Legal Studies and Sociology.

My mix of disciplinaries has given me insights on how the human mind works, how society works, and how to (hopefully) better improve the both of them.

Previously I’ve designed for the University’s International Department, a student club called Crafts4Charity, and was the creative director for Imprint Publications.

If you'd like to chat more about my work or anything else, feel free to message me or connect with me!

Why do I design?

I’ve always loved learning about people and how to make their lives a little bit easier. Studying sociology and law has given me insights to how our legal system has let down a lot of people and I just want to contribute to making this world a better place to be through something I love, design!
