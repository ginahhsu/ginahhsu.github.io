Crochet Lover/ Amateur Yogi / Unapologetically Asian

Just to reintroduce myself, my name is Gina Hsu and I’m a Product Designer. I graduated from the University of Waterloo with a Bachelors of Global Business and Digital Arts and a double minor in Legal Studies and Sociology.

My mix of disciplinaries has given me insights on how the human mind works, how society works, and how to design for both of them.

Previously I’ve designed for the University’s International Department, a student club called Crafts4Charity, and was the Creative Director for Imprint Publications.

If you'd like to chat more about my work or anything else, feel free to message me or connect with me!
